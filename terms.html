EEN Content Poster

Last updated: December 27, 2025

Overview

EEN Content Poster is a self-hosted internal automation tool used solely to upload AI-generated video content to a single TikTok account owned and controlled by the app operator.

Use of TikTok APIs

The app uses TikTok Login Kit (OAuth) and the Content Posting API exclusively to authenticate the account owner and publish content to that same account.

Eligibility

This tool is not available to the public and has no external users.

User Responsibilities

The user is responsible for ensuring all posted content complies with TikTok’s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

No Warranty

The app is provided “as is” for internal use only.

Termination

Access may be revoked at any time by removing authorization in TikTok account settings.
