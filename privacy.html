Privacy Policy

EEN Content Poster

Last updated: December 27, 2025

Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how data is handled by EEN Content Poster, a self-hosted internal automation tool.

Data Collected

The app accesses TikTok OAuth tokens and permissions required to upload and publish content.

Use of Data

Data is used solely to authenticate the TikTok account and operate the content-posting automation.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the operator’s own machine. No third-party storage is used.

Data Sharing

No personal data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

Public Access

The app has no public users, no subscriptions, and no advertising.
